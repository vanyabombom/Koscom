Projects

Project portfolio

Our project portfolio spans nationwide deployments for leading telecom, technology, and enterprise partners. We deliver end-to-end services across residential, commercial, and MDU environments: aerial and underground construction, drop-bury, copper and fiber splicing, FTTH rollouts, and 5G/tower work. Beyond telecom, our teams execute data-center structured cabling, smart-home integrations, security systems and CCTV, access/garage systems, solar panel installs, and EV-charging stations.

Every engagement is run with a safety-led, zero-incident mindset and rigorous quality controls: certified crews, JHAs, utility coordination, as-built/GIS documentation, OTDR and light-level reports, and photo closeouts. From schedule discipline to transparent communication, we deliver reliable outcomes that help partners scale networks and connected infrastructure—on spec, on budget, on time.

Amazon key for business

Scope of work

Security Panel Installation for Amazon Delivery Drivers

Koscom partnered with Amazon Key for Business, a service revolutionizing package delivery.

Our aim was to enhance their delivery infrastructure.

Over a span of 4 months, we installed 150 access panels, providing secure entry points for delivery personnel. A dedicated team of 12 technicians ensured smooth installation. Our efforts not only optimized delivery routes but also bolstered security measures while creating a scalable infrastructure.

Location

NY, NJ, PA, MD, MA, WA, CA

Xfinity Comcast

Scope of work

Residential and MDU Cable Works, Underground/ Drop-Bury, Aerial and Copper Splicing

Comcast Oregon/SW Washington has embarked on a $15 million project to extend its broadband products and services, both residential and business, to the communities of Hubbard and Woodburn, Oregon.

This project brings both communities an access to Xfinity Internet, delivering speeds of 50 megabits per second (Mbps) up to 1.2 gigabits per second (Gbps) for residential customers and up to 10 Gbps for business customers.

Location

OR, WA, MO, CO, UT, MN, MI, IL, IN, PA, NJ, MD, VA, FL, CA
