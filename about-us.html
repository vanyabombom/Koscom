Company

Koscom Networks is a full-service, outsourced partner for broadband cable and wireline telecommunications. We support major operators with end-to-end infrastructure services—premises fulfillment, OSP construction, fiber deployment, and lifecycle maintenance—so networks are designed, built, and sustained to spec.

One Team.
One Goal.

Founded in 2015, we started as field technicians and grew into a turnkey contractor with the same hands-on mindset: customer service, quality craftsmanship, innovation, and safety. Our ability to mobilize crews and equipment across the U.S., align schedules, and hit milestones has earned Koscom Cable a reputation for dependable, on-time delivery.

To keep projects predictable, we run an internal operations platform that unifies work orders, scheduling, material control, QA data (photos, OTDR/light levels), and reporting. This data-driven approach helps teams make informed decisions and navigate unexpected changes with minimal impact on the build.

What We Deliver

We provide end-to-end telecom infrastructure—engineering, OSP build, FTTx deployment, fulfillment, and maintenance—under one accountable team. Safe, on-time, to spec, with measurable quality (OTDR/light levels, photo closeouts, clear as-builts).
