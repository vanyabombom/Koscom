About us

Company

Koscom Networks is a full-service, outsourced partner for broadband cable and wireline telecommunications. We support major operators with end-to-end infrastructure services—premises fulfillment, OSP construction, fiber deployment, and lifecycle maintenance—so networks are designed, built, and sustained to spec.

One Team.
One Goal.

Founded in 2015, we started as field technicians and grew into a turnkey contractor with the same hands-on mindset: customer service, quality craftsmanship, innovation, and safety. Our ability to mobilize crews and equipment across the U.S., align schedules, and hit milestones has earned Koscom Cable a reputation for dependable, on-time delivery.

To keep projects predictable, we run an internal operations platform that unifies work orders, scheduling, material control, QA data (photos, OTDR/light levels), and reporting. This data-driven approach helps teams make informed decisions and navigate unexpected changes with minimal impact on the build.

What regions does Koscom Networks currently cover?

Koscom Networks operates across much of the United States, mobilizing certified aerial and underground crews wherever your project requires. We pair national reach with local permitting know-how to deliver compliant, on-schedule work.For an up-to-date list of active states or upcoming mobilizations, contact our team.

What steps do you take to guarantee timely completion?

We run disciplined, field-proven delivery:

  1. Pre-construction surveys, constructability, and permit tracking
  2. Look-ahead scheduling with material staging and crew assignments
  3. Daily reporting, photo closeouts, and clear change-order control
  4. Safety/JHA briefings and utility coordination before each task
  5. Fiber splicing with OTDR/light-level tests and QA sign-off
  6. Dedicated project management and an escalation path to keep milestones on track
What does Koscom Networks do, and what are its main services?

Koscom Networks provides end-to-end telecommunications infrastructure services:

  • OSP Construction — aerial & underground build-out (make-ready, strand/lasher, conduit, handholes, pole work)
  • Fiber Deployment (FTTx) — placement, splicing, OTDR certification, turn-up
  • Premises Installations — new connects, upgrades, and service calls for residential & commercial sites
  • Drop Bury & HDD — driveway/road/sidewalk crossings via directional boring, plowing, or hand buries with proper restoration
  • Maintenance & Restoration — routine repairs, disconnects/reconnects, and emergency response
  • Utility Locating — precision mark-outs and 811 coordination to prevent strikes
  • Engineering & Permitting — surveys, build-ready plans, permit packages/make-ready, splice schematics, and as-builts
